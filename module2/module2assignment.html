Sunset Set in Motion

Part 1

Getting pricked by the needle in a newly done tapestry, A blood dot surfaces; Which left the Sun of yellow threads on the lake, A wee bit orangish.

Part 2

I smudged the stain with the other thumb, To make it paler; And then I realise with this unintended effort, The sunset looked truer.

Part 3

The piece talks about the beauty of unfolding of unpredictable adventures and the surprises which can spring up. Although, the initial sentiment was not underpinned with a forethought as mentioned above.
